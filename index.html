Name a More Iconic Trio

Alphonso Mango

This mango is considered to be number one in taste and flavor. It holds a sugary-sweet flavor with a buttery and tender texture.

Kent Mango

This mango is mainly produced in Mexico, Ecuador, and Peru. Only being available from December to February. It holds a sweet with sour notes flavoring, with a tender texture and tropical peachy aroma.

Honey Mango

This mango is also produces in Mexico, Ecuador, and Peru. However, is only available from March to July. It holds a sweet and sour flavoring. Has a peach aroma with a small pit and smooth texture. Once ripe it slightly wrinkles and turns a deep yellow color.
